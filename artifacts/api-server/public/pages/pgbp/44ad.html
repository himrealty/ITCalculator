Section 44AD – Presumptive Taxation Scheme
For small businesses with turnover up to ₹2 Crore. Your income is presumed at:
  • 8% of turnover for cash receipts
  • 6% of turnover for digital receipts (if declared)
No need to maintain books of accounts if you opt for this scheme.
⚠️ Important: Once you opt for presumptive taxation, you must continue for 5 years. If you exit earlier, you cannot opt back for next 5 years.
