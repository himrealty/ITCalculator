🚛 Section 44AE – Presumptive Taxation for Transport Business
For assessees owning up to 10 goods vehicles. Income is presumed at:
  • ₹7,500 per month per heavy vehicle (gross vehicle weight > 12,000 kg)
  • ₹4,500 per month per light vehicle (other than heavy)
No need to maintain books of accounts. Applicable for FY 2024-25.
⚠️ Important: This scheme is applicable only if you own NOT MORE THAN 10 VEHICLES. If you own more than 10 vehicles, you cannot opt for presumptive taxation under 44AE.
